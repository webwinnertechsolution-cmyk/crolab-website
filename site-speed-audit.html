What's Slowing Down Your Shopify Store?

Most Shopify stores don't suffer from a single speed issue. Instead, multiple small performance bottlenecks gradually increase loading time.

01
🚀

App Performance

Third-party apps often inject unnecessary JavaScript, CSS, and tracking scripts across every page—even when those features aren't being used.

High Impact
02
🖼️

Image Optimization

Large product images, banners, and collection graphics can significantly increase page weight.

Medium Impact
03
⚡

Render Blocking Resources

CSS files, JavaScript libraries, fonts, and external scripts frequently delay rendering, preventing visitors from interacting with your store quickly.

Critical
04
🧩

Theme Performance

Many Shopify themes accumulate unused CSS, outdated Liquid code, duplicated sections, and unnecessary assets over time.

High Impact
05
🔤

Font Optimization

Custom fonts improve branding but often reduce performance when loaded inefficiently.

Medium Impact
06
📊

Third-Party Integrations

Marketing pixels, analytics platforms, chat widgets, review applications, and personalization tools frequently create hidden performance bottlenecks.

High Impact
