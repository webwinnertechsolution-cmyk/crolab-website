Before You Reach Out

Everything You Need to Know Before Contacting Us

Choosing the right Shopify growth partner is an important decision. Below are answers to some of the most common questions we receive from ecommerce businesses before starting a project.

Which Shopify service is right for my business? +

If you're unsure where to start, our Shopify CRO Audit is usually the best first step. It provides a complete evaluation of your customer journey, identifies conversion barriers, and often highlights SEO, performance, and usability issues at the same time. From there, we can recommend whether you would benefit from a dedicated SEO Audit, Site Speed Audit, or an ongoing Shopify Growth Retainer.

Do you only work with Shopify stores? +

Yes. We specialize exclusively in Shopify and Shopify Plus. By focusing on a single ecommerce platform, we can provide deeper expertise in conversion optimization, technical SEO, performance improvements, UX optimization, and Shopify development than agencies that work across multiple platforms.

What happens after I submit the form? +

After receiving your enquiry, one of our Shopify specialists reviews your store, your business goals, and the information you've provided. Within approximately 36 business hours, we'll contact you with our recommendations, explain the most suitable service for your requirements, and outline the next steps for moving your project forward.

Can you implement the recommendations from my audit? +

Absolutely. After completing your Shopify CRO Audit, SEO Audit, or Site Speed Audit, our team can implement every recommendation through one-off development work or one of our monthly Shopify Growth Retainers.

Do you work with international Shopify businesses? +

Yes. We work with Shopify merchants worldwide, from growing ecommerce startups to established Shopify Plus brands, helping them improve search visibility, website performance, and conversion rates regardless of their location.
