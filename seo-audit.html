What's Included

What's Included in Our SEO Audit

A successful Shopify SEO strategy goes far beyond optimizing page titles and adding keywords. Search engines evaluate hundreds of technical and content-related factors before deciding how your products and collections should rank.

01

Site Architecture & Crawlability

A strong website structure helps search engines discover, crawl, and understand every important page on your Shopify store. Poor navigation, duplicate URLs, broken links, unnecessary redirects, and crawl errors can reduce visibility and waste valuable crawl budget.

02

On-Page SEO Optimization

Every page on your Shopify store should clearly communicate its purpose to both search engines and customers. We review every critical on-page SEO element to improve keyword relevance, click-through rates, and search visibility.

03

Structured Data & Schema Markup

Schema markup helps search engines better understand your products, reviews, pricing, breadcrumbs, and business information. Proper implementation can improve rich search results while increasing visibility across Google and AI-powered search experiences.

04

Keyword & Content Gap Analysis

Ranking higher requires more than technical optimization. Your content must answer customer questions while targeting valuable search intent.

05

AI Search Optimization

Search is changing rapidly with AI-powered platforms becoming an important discovery channel. We evaluate whether your Shopify content is structured clearly enough for AI systems to understand, summarize, and recommend your products.

06

Core Web Vitals & Technical Performance

Website speed and technical performance influence both user experience and search rankings. We identify performance bottlenecks that reduce crawl efficiency, increase bounce rates, and negatively affect conversions.
