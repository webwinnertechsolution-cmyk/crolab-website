What is included in a Shopify retainer?

›
›A Shopify retainer provides ongoing access to a dedicated team of Shopify developers, CRO specialists, SEO experts, and UX professionals who continuously improve your store through technical updates, conversion optimization, SEO enhancements, performance improvements, and strategic consulting.
?

Is there a long-term contract?

›
›No. All of our Shopify retainers operate on a flexible month-to-month basis. You can upgrade, downgrade, pause, or cancel your plan as your business requirements change.
?

How does the 7-day trial work?

›
›Your trial gives you full access to your selected retainer plan, allowing you to experience our workflow, communication, and implementation process before committing to a monthly subscription.

Can you implement recommendations from previous audits?

›
›Absolutely. Many of our clients begin with a Shopify CRO Audit, SEO Audit, or Site Speed Audit before moving onto a monthly retainer. Our team implements every recommendation and continues optimizing your store over time.

Do you support Shopify Plus?

›Yes. We work with both standard Shopify and Shopify Plus stores, providing advanced development, checkout optimization, automation, and enterprise-level growth strategies.
