Anatomy of a Conversion Lift

How we helped a 7-figure DTC brand stop abandoning carts and scale their paid traffic profitably.

Apparel Brand • Shopify Plus

Removing friction on the path to purchase

This brand had high top-of-funnel traffic, but mobile users were bouncing at the Add-to-Cart stage. Our CRO audit revealed that sticky promotional banners and a cluttered variants selector were pushing the primary CTA below the fold.

The Fix: We redesigned the product page to feature a floating sticky ATC button on mobile, simplified the variant swatches, and injected dynamic trust badges below the price.
