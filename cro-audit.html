End-to-End CRO Audit

Our Shopify CRO Audit reviews your entire customer journey to uncover conversion blockers, reduce friction, improve user experience, build trust, and help turn more visitors into paying customers.

Homepage & Navigation

Your homepage creates the first impression of your brand. We evaluate messaging clarity, navigation structure, visual hierarchy, featured collections, promotional content, search functionality, and user pathways to ensure visitors immediately understand what you sell and where to go next.

Collection Pages

Collection pages should help customers discover products quickly without becoming overwhelmed. We review filtering options, sorting functionality, product organization, category structure, and overall browsing experience to improve product discovery and encourage deeper engagement.

Product Pages

Product pages are where buying decisions happen. We evaluate product descriptions, imagery, pricing presentation, trust badges, reviews, urgency messaging, product variants, FAQs, shipping information, and add-to-cart functionality to remove friction and improve purchase confidence.

Cart & Checkout

Even small checkout issues can significantly impact revenue. We identify friction throughout the checkout experience, including unexpected costs, confusing forms, coupon fields, payment options, shipping transparency, and cart usability to reduce abandonment and increase completed purchases.

Mobile User Experience

More than half of ecommerce traffic comes from mobile devices. We thoroughly evaluate your mobile shopping experience, ensuring every page is optimized for smaller screens, faster interactions, and seamless purchasing.

Trust & Conversion Signals

Customers need confidence before making a purchase. We analyze every trust-building element across your store, including customer reviews, testimonials, guarantees, return policies, contact information, payment security indicators, and social proof to strengthen buying confidence.
